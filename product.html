Sustainable Plant Nutrition

We produce calcium nitrate fertilizer using only water, air and electricity from solar panels. To make the fertilizer we use high voltages to produce air plasma which rips apart nitrogen molecules in air and binds them in water.

Our production method relies completely on renewable energy - be it solar, wind or hydro. We leave no mark on the environment and we are proud of it.

Our production plants are small and we set them up as close as possible to our customers so no unnecessary transport is needed.
