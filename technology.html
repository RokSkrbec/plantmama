How does it work?

Our technology is based on decades of academic research, using only water, air, and electricity to create powerful plant nutrition. The process mimics nature's own way of enriching soil through lightning storms.

To make the nitrogen fertilizer, we use a plasma reactor that creates electric arcs. This plasma has such highly concentrated energy that it rips apart nitrogen molecules in the air, which then bind with water to form nitrates - the essential nutrient for plants.

The same process occurs naturally during thunderstorms when lightning breaks apart air molecules, which then combine with raindrops. We've simply found a way to harness this natural process efficiently and sustainably.
